You must get the fox, chicken, and sack of grain across the wide river. The trouble is, his boat is old and leaky, and you can only take one across at a time. If you leave the fox with the chicken, the chicken will be eaten. If you leave the chicken with the grain, the chicken will eat the grain.
Click on the fox, chicken, or sacks of corn to move them into or out of the boat. Click on the boat to cross the river.
